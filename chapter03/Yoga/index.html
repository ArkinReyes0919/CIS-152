Yoga Classes

Gentle Hatha Yoga
Intended for beginners and anyone wishing a grounded foundation in the practice of yoga, this 60 min class and slow movement focuses on asana (proper alignment and posture). Pranayama (breath work), and guided meditation to faster your mind and body connection.
Vinyasa Yoga
Although designed for intermediate to advance students, beginners are welcome to sample this 60 minute class that focuses on breath-synchronize movement — you will inhale and exhale as you flow energetically through yoga poses.
Restorative Yoga
This 90 min class features very slow movement and long poses that are supported by a chair or wall. This calming, restorative experience is suitable for student of any level of experience. This practice can be a perfect way to help rehabilitate an injury
