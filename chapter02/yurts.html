The Yurts at Pacific Trails

What is a yurt?
Our amazing yurts are structure thats made four feet off the ground. its usually enclose with canvas walls , and wooden wall to protect from foreign wild life such as animals. It also has roof dome which you can also access to see the view safely within your dome.
How are the yurts finish
each of our yurts usually comes with a queen size bed with down quilt and a gas fire stove. Not only, that but it also comes electricity, and sink with hot or cold water. For you to access shower, and restroom, You will have to visit the facility which are located in the lodge.
What should i bring?
Most of our guest in this place usually bring light layer clothes do to its changing weather, a pair of flash light, and a sense of adventure to enjoy the full experience of the Resort.
